What This Hub Is For

This resource hub is designed to be a connection between the students of Gwinnett County and the various resources available to them. Whether it's finding a tutor, looking for scholarships, or just finding fun activities to do, this hub is meant to be a one-stop shop for all things that students may need. The goal is to make it easier for students to find the resources they need and to encourage them to take advantage of the opportunities available to them, while connecting the community in a modern fashion.
